Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

BudgetPro ("the app") is a personal income and expense tracker. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and how you can control or delete it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address and password you use to sign up, and an optional display name.
  • Financial data you enter: transaction amounts, categories, dates, notes, and the "paid by" label you choose to enter; budget limits you set; and savings goals and contributions you log.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or device identifiers, and the app contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.

How your information is used

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core features to you: authenticating your account, displaying your transactions and budgets, and generating the charts, insights, and goal-progress views inside the app. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security enabled, meaning the database itself enforces that your account can only ever read or write your own data — no other user of the app can access it. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

Sharing your information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal or financial data to third parties. Your data is only accessible to you through your authenticated session, and to the underlying infrastructure provider (Supabase) that hosts the database on our behalf.

Your controls

  • You can edit or delete any individual transaction, budget, or goal at any time from within the app.
  • You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Delete my account and all data. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • You can also request account deletion by emailing us at the address below.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your profile, transactions, budgets, goals, and goal contributions are deleted immediately and permanently.

Children's privacy

BudgetPro is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a revised "last updated" date.
