## Full description (max 4000 characters)
BudgetPro is a clean, minimal money tracker that helps you understand exactly where your money goes — and helps you save more of it.

**Track every rupee**
Log income and expenses in seconds, tagged by category, date, and who it was for. See your full transaction history any time, and export it whenever you need to.

**Budgets that keep you honest**
Set a monthly limit for each spending category and watch your progress in real time, with clear warnings before you go over.

**An analytics dashboard that tells the story of your money**
Beyond simple totals, BudgetPro turns your data into a narrative: your heaviest spending month, your top categories, how essential vs. discretionary spending breaks down, your biggest single expenses, and your income-vs-expense trend over time — all in one place.

**Savings goals**
Set a target for anything you're saving toward — a car, a trip, an emergency fund — and log contributions as you go. Watch your progress bar fill up.

**Smart, private insights**
BudgetPro surfaces rule-based tips from your own spending patterns — no ads, no data mining, no gimmicks.

**Built with privacy in mind**
Your data is yours. It's stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties. You can delete any entry, or your entire account and all its data, at any time.

Whether you're trying to build a habit of tracking your spending, save for something specific, or just get a clearer picture of your finances, BudgetPro keeps it simple, fast, and entirely under your control.
